WHAT IS PAYREALITY?
==================================================

PayReality is an enterprise infrastructure platform for delegated authority and decision governance for autonomous AI systems.

As AI evolves from copilots into autonomous agents capable of executing real business actions, organizations face a new problem.

Traditional Identity and Access Management (IAM) answers:

"Who is the agent?"

But it cannot answer:

"What authority has been delegated to that agent?"

Organizations already have mature Delegation of Authority frameworks governing:

• Financial approvals
• Procurement
• Vendor onboarding
• HR actions
• Operational decisions
• Compliance approvals

These frameworks were designed for humans.

PayReality translates those existing human delegation frameworks into machine-enforceable authority that autonomous AI systems must satisfy BEFORE executing actions.

The platform evaluates every action against governance policies, authority boundaries, contextual risk and approval rules before execution occurs.

Every decision is recorded with verifiable evidence and complete auditability.

PayReality does NOT replace AI.

It enables organizations to safely deploy autonomous AI.
